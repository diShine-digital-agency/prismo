# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who contributes to or interacts with Prismo, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code reviews.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Accept constructive criticism.
- Prioritize what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to kevin@dishine.it. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly. Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
